Swiss Table

Setup

New tournament

Four rounds. Players are grouped into tables each round; a player's placement at their table earns Swiss points, and cumulative Swiss points — not raw game scores — decide the overall standings and the next round's pairings.

If the player count doesn't divide evenly, tables in a round will differ by at most one seat.

Swiss Pts per table = 1st: 4 · 2nd: 2 · 3rd: 1 · 4th: 0 · ties share the higher placement · Diff = score − table's top score
